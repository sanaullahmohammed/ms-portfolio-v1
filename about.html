About Me

Hey, I'm Mohammed.

I'm a Software Design Engineer at Achieve.ai m, working on the development of 5 high-traffic websites and mobile applications that cater to millions of users preparing for competitive exams.

As a child, I was captivated by the magic of video games and curious about how they were built. As I grew older, my interest in technology expanded, and I became fascinated with fixing computer hardware and understanding how operating systems functioned. This marked the beginning of my tech journey.

Nowadays, I love creating websites and applications using various stacks, whether it's as simple as a single HTML file or as complex as a MERN, .NET, or T3 stack. Beyond work, I enjoy building personal projects that allow me to delve deeper into the technologies that intrigue me and develop innovative solutions. Lately, I have been focusing on working with Ethereum blockchain and webRTC to build some exciting applications.

In addition to my passion for technology, I also believe in the importance of physical and mental fitness. I enjoy powerlifting and cycling, which helps me clear my mind and energizes me for the day ahead.
